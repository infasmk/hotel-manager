# InnLedger

InnLedger is a private, admin-only hotel management and accounting system designed to help hotel owners track daily income, daily expenses, staff attendance, salaries, advances, and monthly financial summaries.

This application is built strictly for internal use and focuses on accuracy, simplicity, and fast daily operations.

---

## Purpose

Managing hotel finances manually can lead to errors, confusion, and wasted time.  
InnLedger provides a single dashboard to:

- Track daily income and expenses
- Monitor staff attendance
- Manage staff salary and advances
- View month-wise profit and loss
- Maintain clear financial records

---

## User Access

- Admin (Hotel Owner)
- Secure login required
- No public or customer access
- Single-owner control system

---

## Features

### Dashboard
- Today’s income
- Today’s expenses
- Net profit (auto-calculated)
- Staff present / absent count
- Monthly summary view

### Daily Income
- Date-based income entry
- Multiple income sources
- Amount and notes
- Edit and delete entries
- Automatic daily total

### Daily Expenses
- Date-based expense entry
- Expense categories
- Payment mode (cash / online)
- Amount and notes
- Automatic expense total

### Staff Management
- Staff name and role
- Monthly salary
- Joining date
- Attendance (present / absent)

### Salary & Advance Tracking
- Advance payments record
- Salary paid history
- Remaining salary auto-calculation
- Monthly staff expense summary

### Monthly Reports
- Total income per month
- Total expenses per month
- Net profit or loss
- Salary totals
- Clear financial overview
